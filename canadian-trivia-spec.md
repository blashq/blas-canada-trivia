# 🍁 BLAS Canada Day Trivia — Full Build Spec

**Event:** BLAS team Canada Day celebration (tomorrow)
**Players:** 16 participants in **4 teams of 4** · Ayaz hosts (not a player)
**Devices:** one **laptop per team** (2–4 teams supported) + Ayaz's private host panel
**Length:** ~50–55 min of play, comfortably inside an hour
**Look & feel:** bold **Canada Day** (flag red + white, maple-leaf motifs) with **BLAS teal accent + logo**

---

## The three surfaces

1. **Big screen** (`/present`, fullscreen on the projector machine) — questions, timers, answer reveals, educational blurbs, branding. **Full blackout on anything competitive:** no scores, no standings, no team callouts. Only an anonymous tally ("2 of 4 teams got it right").
2. **Team view** (`/play`, one per laptop) — enter room code → pick team name + colour/maple-leaf avatar → answer. Teams see only their own picks (and the wager slider at the finale).
3. **Host panel** (`/host`, Ayaz's phone or laptop, never projected) — the live leaderboard with **real numbers**, who answered what, all controls, and Test Mode.

---

## Global rules (every round)

- **No abstaining anywhere.** A no-answer is scored exactly like a wrong answer.
- **The timer always hard-locks entries** at zero. Ayaz's "tap to reveal" only controls *when the answer is shown*, never when entries close.
- **Score blackout on the big screen** all game long — only the anonymous "X of N got it right" appears. Real totals live on the host panel and are revealed to everyone only at the very end.
- **Every round opens with a rules-and-points intro card** (name, how it works, scoring) — Ayaz taps to begin.
- Reveals are **host-tapped** unless noted (rapid-fire and the clue drips are timer-driven).

---

## Running order & round specs

### 1. Which Province/Territory Is This? (maps)
- Map silhouette on screen; 4 options = correct + a mix of **real** provinces/territories and **made-up Canadian-sounding** decoys, blended per question for maximum confusion.
- **7 questions · 20-sec timer · +1 / −1**
- Reveal: correct option highlights in **Canadian red**, plus the province's **flag & name**.

### 2. Provincial & Territorial Capitals
- 4 options, all **real recognizable cities clustered from the same province** (e.g., Victoria / Vancouver / Surrey / Kelowna) so familiarity creates doubt.
- **7 questions · 20-sec · +1 / −1**
- Reveal: correct capital with the province's **flag & name**.

### 3. Spot the Canadian
- **4 photos**, teams **select everyone who's Canadian** (1–4 of them are; at least one always is). **Must select at least one.**
- **5 questions · 30-sec · +1 per correct pick / −1 per wrong pick** (can go negative).
- Reveal: **non-Canadians blur out**, **Canadians get a Canadian-red border**.
- *Photos supplied by Ayaz (Canadians + non-Canadian decoys).*

### 4. Canadian Slang
- Slang term on screen → **4 plausible, tricky definitions** (believable, not comedic — tests who actually knows).
- **7 questions · 20-sec · +1 / −1**
- Reveal: correct definition + a short **origin/history blurb** (educational).

### 5. Name This Canadian City (clue drip)
- **6 cities.** Clues drip **automatically every 10 sec**: clue 1 @ 0:00 → clue 2 @ 0:10 → clue 3 @ 0:20 → closes @ 0:30.
- Correct while clue 1 shows = **+3**, clue 2 = **+2**, clue 3 = **+1**; **wrong or no answer = −1**.
- **Selection is final — no undo**, even as new clues appear.
- **Recycled-city mind-game:** a past correct answer returns as the correct answer again at least once, plus reused decoys, to punish "it can't be that one again" thinking.

### 6. Canadian or Not? (rapid-fire)
- **20 true/false statements** — surprising Canadians, Canadian inventions, and **globally famous companies people don't realize are Canadian** (McCain, Four Seasons, Lululemon, Shopify, BlackBerry, Cirque du Soleil…) plus "feels Canadian but isn't" traps (North Face, Patagonia).
- **10 sec each, auto-fires through, no mid-round reveals · +1 / −1**
- **End-of-round recap:** Ayaz taps through all 20 one at a time — correct answer + **educational one-liner** + the anonymous tally.

### 7. General Grab-Bag
- Broad Canadiana (history, geography, pop culture, sports, food), multiple choice.
- **8 questions · 20-sec · +2 / −1** (stakes rising late).
- Reveal + educational blurb.

### 8. You Should Know This, BLAS! (work round)
- Canadian tax & finance — **mixed formats in one round**: multiple choice, true/false, select-all, and a **bank clue-drip** (2 clues → guess from RBC / BMO / Scotia / TD / CIBC).
- **8 questions (room to grow) · 15-sec** (non-drip); bank drip clues @ 0:00 / 0:10, closes @ 0:20.
- **4× scoring:** MC/TF **+4 / −2**; select-all **+4 per right pick / −2 per wrong** (pick ≥1); **bank drip clue 1 = +8, clue 2 = +4**; wrong / no answer = **−2** everywhere.
- Sample content: how many provinces/territories charge no PST; lowest-total-tax province; "what's the sales tax rate in [province]?"; corp T2 deadline from a year-end; annual GST/HST filing deadline; bank clue-drips.
- Reveal + educational blurb. **Every rate, threshold, and deadline fact-checked against current CRA / provincial sources** before go-live.

### 9. 🍁 Final Wager
- Category shown first → each team sets a **secret slider wager (0–100%)** of their **hidden** total → the question reveals: **"What does the word *Canada* mean?"** (answer: *village / settlement*, from the Iroquoian *kanata*).
- **Correct = +your wager · Wrong = −your wager.**
- **Dramatic reveal, last place → first**, each team's card flipping to show wager, pick, and swing.
- Then the **section-by-section scorecard** fills in and resolves into the **final leaderboard** — the first time any real numbers appear all night. Winner crowned with falling maple leaves + BLAS branding.

## Join, lobby & reset
- **No accounts** — teams enter a room code, pick a name + colour/avatar, and they're in.
- **Flexible 2–4 teams** — game and scoreboard adapt.
- **Lobby** on the host panel shows teams as they join; Ayaz hits **Start Game**; can **remove a team** (clears test entries).
- **Reset / New Game** button wipes all teams, answers, and scores back to a fresh lobby (same room code/links) — built for repeated testing.

## Test Mode (host panel only, never projected)
- **End-timer button** — close any question instantly instead of waiting.
- **Jump to any round.**
- **Skip / Next** past questions and reveals.
- **Auto-answer bots** — test teams auto-submit so Ayaz can run a full game solo; toggle off to test the real laptop experience.

---

## Tech & isolation
- **Supabase** (real-time backend) + **Vercel** (hosting) — brand-new, standalone projects for this game only, not linked to any BLAS workplace app, and **fully deletable** after the event (teardown steps provided).
- Public link for the team laptops; no installs.
